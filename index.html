About Me

About PG :)

My name is Prateek Gupta and I'm a full time Software Engineer and a hardcore Gamer currently working with Grofers India Pvt Ltd I completed B. Tech (Information Technology) from NIT Bhopal in 2012.

From Hobby to Profession

I am basically from Ujjain (M.P.), India and I came up from a small family. No one in my family ever had any interest in computers. Even me too didn't like it very much except playing games on it. But it was when I made a simple calculator using Visual Basic in 2006, I kind of got interested in this and the very same day I decided that I would be a software engineer. Ever since writing my first program and manipulating it to produce a desired output, I have been obsessed with the idea of using software to solve practical problems. I believe in the power of programming to transform and improve the lives of people around the world.

I started my career as an Android developer with the B2B innovation team in Samsung Research Institute Noida. Here I worked on a variety of projects targeting multiple geographical regions. I got 2 patents filed during this time. Post this, I worked with CarDekho.com on their consumer app and majorly contributed to redesign the home screen and car details screen.

I tried my hands on building a few games on Android. Some of my applications in playstore are Race to the top, My KPI Filler and MovieMania Hangman. All apps that I developed in my personal time can be found here.

I joined Grofers as an Android developer in May 2016 and here I started working on backend and frontend development also. I built the warehouse tech team in Grofers starting with only 2 people in the team in 2016. We created a warehouse management system (phoenix) from scratch that was capable of running offline. During the last 4 years the journey of phoenix reached from running on android tablets to AWS cloud. I worked closely with the IT team and we set up MPLS connections across our warehouses to increase the reliability of internet connection in the warehouses.

Currently I am working with Grofers as an Engineering Manager and my primary responsibilities are to,
  • Work with Product team as a partner in getting business results delivered for the team's goals
  • Communicate technical concepts to business stakeholders as well as communicate business objectives to the technical team
  • Identify areas of strategic technical debt and provide cost/benefit analysis for eliminating this debt
  • Identify and clear roadblocks for the team
  • Manage team members with different skill sets and technical areas of focus


I like managing people and people near me say that I am a very patient listener so they like to talk to me about their problems. Currently I am managing a team of 14 people including 8 developers, 4 QA and 2 product solution engineers.

Outside of work, I like to play my Xbox and go for long drives in my car :)
